# PresenceCraft
Adds Discord Rich Presence support to Minecraft.

## Features
- Shows your current dimension on Discord.
- Shows your current survival day count.
- Updates status when:
    - Viewing menus
    - Pausing your game
    - Actively playing in the world.
- Shows details depending on what you are doing:
    - Singleplayer - 'Singleplayer'
    - Joining a LAN World - 'Multiplayer (LAN)'
    - Hosting a LAN World - 'Multiplayer (Hosting LAN)'
    - Playing on a Server - 'Multiplayer (Server)'
    - Playing on a Realm - 'Multiplayer (Realm)'
- (Optional) Shows the server IP of the server you are playing on.
- Shows player count in multiplayer worlds.
- Uses your Minecraft Avatar as the Small Image in Discord's RPC
- Shows your Minecraft username in the Small Image text.
- Uses your Minecraft version and current dimension in the Rich Presence tooltip.
- Has a fully customizable Discord Rich Presence.

## Settings
- **Enable Discord RPC**: Whether Discord Rich Presence is enabled.
- **Show Server IP**: Whether to show the IP of the server you are playing on (disabled by default).
- **Show Player Count**: Whether to show the number of players in a multiplayer world.
- **Show Player Avatar**: Whether to use your Minecraft avatar as the Small Image.
- **Show Minecraft Version**: Whether to show your Minecraft version in the Rich Presence image tooltip.
- **Show Dimension**: Whether to show your current dimension in your Rich Presence.
- **Show Survival Day**: Whether to show your survival day count.
